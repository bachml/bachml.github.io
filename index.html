About Me

Q I am a PhD Candidate at Australian Artificial Intelligence Institute, University Technology Sydney, supervised by Prof. Jie Lu. I obtained M.Phil degree from The University of Melbourne, supervised by Prof. James Bailey.

Before joining UTS, I worked as a software engineer at Huawei and SenseTime. My past work involved integrating artificial intelligence and computer vision into software products.

Currently, my primary research interests are machine learning and recommendation systems.
